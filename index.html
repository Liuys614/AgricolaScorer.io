Agricola Scorer

The Ultimate Scoring Tool for Agricola Board Game

Agricola Scorer is a simple yet powerful tool designed specifically for Agricola board game enthusiasts. Say goodbye to manual calculations and score sheet errors!

Key Features:

  • Easy and intuitive scoring for all Agricola game categories
  • Support for both Family Game and Standard Game scoring
  • Save and track your game history
  • Compare scores with friends
  • Statistics to track your improvement over time
  • Clean, user-friendly interface

Whether you're a casual player or a serious Agricola competitor, this app will enhance your gaming experience by making the scoring process quick, accurate, and hassle-free.

Available for both iOS and Android devices.
